Continued Optimism for swedish companies

The spring 2017 CF0 Survey reveals a clearly more positive sentiment among large Swedish companies. The overall CFO index shows, after a decline in the two latest surveys, a good jump to 58.2 from 54.1 in 2016.

This is the highest level since spring 2011, Except for slightly reduced lending willingness, the other subindexes - business climate, nancial position and counterparty risk - improved significantly. In the last survey it was clear that CFO's took into account the still uncertain macro suituation after several major events during the course of 2016.
